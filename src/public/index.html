About

Tammy

Hello! My name is Tammy Barrett and I am the owner, designer & general contractor of Barrett Designs, Inc., located in Edmond, Oklahoma. I am proud to be an Oklahoma native!

For the past 22 years, I have worked all over the country spreading the joy of breathing new life into tired spaces as well as creating new ones. I have always had a passion for creativity with textiels and color, and in fact almost majored in Fashion Merchandising, but eventually fellin love with interior design. I work closely with each client to create beautiful spaces that reflect their unique personalities.

When not obsessing over my projects, you can find me shopping for furniture & accessories, working out, or enjoying tmie with my husband of over 30 years.

Services

Welcome to Barrett Designs! We offer more than just designing beautiful spaces - we bring them to life! As a full-service interior design and general contracting firm for both residential and commercial spaces, we offer a seamless start-to-finish approach that transforms your home or office with both style and precision. From the initial concept to final installation, we manage every detail, ensuring a smooth stress-free experience.

This design approach means you don't have to juggle multiple contractors - we take care of everything. You get a streamlined process, clear communication, and exceptional results, all in one place.

Let's bring your dream space to life - designed for the way you live!


RENOVATIONS
Barrett Designs has an experienced team of tradesmen to help create unique remodel designs. Whether refreshing a single space or reimagining your entire interior, our design expertise ensures a seamless transformation that enhances both function and beauty.
During your project, we manage everything including the electrical work, painting, wood works and more.
Every renovation is tailored to your vision, ensuring your home reflects your personal style while increasing comfort and value.


NEW CONSTRUCTION
If you have a builder in place to build your new home, I can help you with everything else!
Through a series of consultations and selectino meetings, I can assist you with your floor plan and/or architectural drawings, cabinet design, paint colors, flooring, tile, countertops, lighting & hardware decisions.
The devil is in the details, and it is these details that can make your home look thoughtfully and intentionally designed.
